Profile

Tim Chisholm is a multi-disciplinary artist, writer and entrepreneur. He is a classically trained theatre actor and director, has worked professionally in theatre and film for ten years, and has also been employed as a sportswriter since 2005 covering the Toronto Raptors basketball club for Bell, TSN and ESPN TrueHoop Network Blog Raptors Republic. He is currently running his startup Shakespeare at Play.

Selected Experience

  • Founder/CEO, atplay; Technology - 2013-Present

    Founded atplay, a how-to and DIY aggregation site that features the best instructional material from major brands as well as a vast library of user-generated material. Tim designs and oversees business and product strategy, as well as overseeing the workforce and financial health of the company.

  • Founder/Director, Shakespeare at Play; Theatre/Film - 2012-Present

    Founded Shakespeare at Play, an interactive mobile app that combines Shakespeare's texts with full-video productions of his works. Works as a director on all filmed productions, which to date have included Romeo & Juliet (released March 2013), Macbeth (released July 2013), Hamlet (released March 2014) and A Midsummer Night's Dream (released September 2014)

  • Advisor, Toronto Film School; Film - 2012-2014

    Advised the upper administration to shape curriculum for the acting program and worked to find ways to best prepare actors for the working world upon their graduation.

  • Co-Founder/Artistic Director, Theatre in the Red; Theatre - 2009-2011

    Worked closely with several Toronto-based writers in workshopping and dramaturging their works and preparing them for theatrical presentation.

  • Director, Come Tumbling Down (Theatre in the Red); Theatre - 2010

    Staged independently at Lower Ossington Theatre and subsequently as part of the 2010 Toronto Fringe Festival. Also worked at Theatre in the Red at the board level.

  • Asst. Director, From Here to Africville and Once a Flame (Theatre in the Red); Theatre - 2010

  • Columnist, The Sports Network (TSN); Journalism - 2005-2012

    Wrote Three In The Key, an NBA-centric column for TSN.ca, with a particular focus on the Toronto Raptors. Content included analysis, editorial and interviews.

Education

University of Toronto; DRAMA; Toronto, ON - 2001-2005

Skills
